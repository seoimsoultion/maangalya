About Park Avenue

Your home in the lap of nature. You will be living close to nature at Maangalya Park Avenue since we have done everything possible to reduce our carbon footprint and create a scenic environment with lush greenery and landscaped surroundings that you will feel proud to call your home. These 3 bhk ultra luxurious BBMP & BDA approved apartments are located in prime location and close to well-known malls, hospitals, IT parks, and educational institutes and have everything to match your lifestyle, right from premium amenities to complete privacy, ensured by the fact that there are no common walls. Moreover, all the apartments are Vaastu complaint and offer maximum amount of light and ventilation in each apartment.

As part of our initiative towards green living, we have implemented advanced technology in all the bathrooms at Maangalya Park Avenue that helps in saving as much as 50% of water that is otherwise wasted while we perform different activities such as bathing or house cleaning. In addition, we have decided to plant one sapling for every home at Maangalya Park Avenue. This means, we will be able to add 144 trees for each of the 144 apartments at this already green project and all the saplings will be named after respective head of families, to remind your future generations of the part you have played in saving our environment.

Specifications

    STRUCTURE
  • Frame Structure: RCC frame structure with masonry partitions.
  • Masonry: High strength concrete blocks for external walls 8” and internal walls 4” masonry with plaster shall be provided.
  • PLASTERING: Internal walls with cement mortar, external walls with cement mortar and sponge finish.
    FLOORING & DADO
  • Tiles: White Horse vitrified tiles 2x2 ft for living, dining, bedrooms and rustic finish vitrified tiles for kitchen, utility & balcony.
  • Flooring: Lobby and staircase will have polished granite flooring.
  • Kitchen & Utility: Granite counter with CARYSIL Sink and DADO tiles up to roof height.
    SANITARY FIXTURES
  • Water Closets: EWC of Jaquar make.
  • Taps, Showers & Faucets:Jaquar make.
  • Washing Machine Point: One cold water inlet & drainage outlet for a washing machine.
  • Plumbing: All water supply lines, sewage lines & drainage lines of CPVC & PVC of SUPREME/ASHIRVAD make.
    ELECTRICAL
  • Wiring: Wiring & Cables from HAVELLS.
  • Switches: All switches & sockets shall be of CRABTREE.
  • Phone & TV Point: One telephone & television point each shall be provided in living and all the bedrooms.
  • Power Connection: A power connection of 5 KVA shall be provided to each flat.
    PAINT
  • Internal walls: Two coats of high-quality putty as well as BERGER plastic emulsion washable paint specifically for the interior walls.
  • Exterior walls: BERGER texture paint along with two coats of superior weather coat paint according to elevation requirements for all the exterior walls.
    DOORS & ACCESSORIES
  • Safety and security of your home depends a lot upon how good or bad the doors, windows, and accessories are. As such at Maangalya Park Avenue we have made sure that all the doors frames as well as shutters will be of BST with imported timber along with Godrej locks, hinges & tower bolts
