About Park Avenue

Worldwide, humankind has been paying a heavy price for the deforestation of land and emission of green house gases. The havoc nature’s fury has caused in Karnataka and neighbouring states is a pointer to the warnings scientists had been issuing about the effects of global warming.

To stem the root and do our bit in reducing the carbon foot prints by enhancing the greenery around us, Team Maangalya has come up with a unique idea. One sapling would be planted at the premises of Maangalya Park Avenue on behalf of each family staying in the apartment. What’s more, this sapling would be named after the head of the family! As there are 144 apartment units, a total of 144 trees would be added to the lush greenery already gracing Maangalya!

Specifications

    STRUCTURE
  • RCC frame structure with masonry partitions.
  • l MASONRY: High strength concrete blocks for external walls 8” and internal walls 4” masonry with plaster shall be provided.
  • PLASTERING: Internal walls with cement mortar, external walls with cement mortar and sponge finish.
    FLOORING & DADO
  • White Horse vitrified tiles 2x2 ft for Living, Dining, Bedrooms and Rustic finish vitrified tiles for Kitchen, Utility & balcony along with 3 mm spacers with MYK LATICRETE EPOXY GROUT .
  • l Lobby will have polished granite flooring, same for staircase with brushed stainless steel railing.
  • Kitchen & Utility: Granite counter with CARYSIL Sink & DADO tiles up to roof height.
    SANITARY FIXTURES
  • Water Closets: EWC of Jaquar make .
  • Wash Basins: Jaquar make .
  • Taps, Showers & Faucets: From Jaquar make .
  • Washing Machine Point: One cold water inlet and drainage outlet for a wh as ing machine shall be provided in the Utility.
  • Plumbing: All water supply lines, sewage lines and drainage lines shall be in CPVC & PVC of SUPREME/ASHIRVAD make.
    ELECTRICAL
  • Wiring & Cables shall be of HAVELLS.
  • Switches: All switches & sockets shall be of CRABTREE.
  • One telephone and television point each shall be provided in living and all the bedrooms.
  • A power connection of 5 KVA shall be provided to each flat.
    PAINT
  • l Internal walls: 2 coats of putty and BERGER Plastic emulsion washable paint.
  • Exterior walls: BERGER texture along with 2 coats of weather coat paint according to elevation requirements.
    DOORS & ACCESSORIES
  • l All doors frames & shutters will be of BST with imported timber along with Godrej locks, hinges & tower bolts.
